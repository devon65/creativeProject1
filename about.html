About us

Devon Howard and Michael Briggs met in a computer science class whilst attending Brigham
Young University. During the long hours of their pair programming assignments, Michael
and Devon ate pizza to fuel their coding escapades. The local choices weren't up to their
standards, so they decided to make their own pizza. The pizza was so great that they decided to sell it.

Fueled by Pizza. Powered by laughter.
